Boots On The Ground For 20 Years

Raising my boys and working in this beautiful city for over twenty years, I have actively advocated for local businesses, our families, and schools. I've been honored to serve on several city committies, as well as helping to renew the police levy to maintain a healthy balance between growth and security. As our city continues to expand, my vision is to help facilitate growth in such a way that I preserve the history and livability of our city, with an eye towards a fiscally conservative future. I am excited for the opportunity to serve as your City Councilor, and I humbly ask for your vote.
